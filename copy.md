## Risks ("The Risks of a Neglected Dryer Vent")

**H2:** The Risks of a Neglected Dryer Vent

**Intro:** A clogged dryer vent is more than an inconvenience — it's a serious safety hazard that affects your home, health, and wallet.

**Cards:**

- **Fire Hazard** — Lint buildup causes your dryer to overheat — the leading cause of dryer fires. Clean vents dramatically cut that risk.
- **Reduced Efficiency** — About 75% of lint bypasses the lint trap and collects in the duct, restricting airflow and doubling drying time.
- **Higher Energy Bills** — A clogged vent forces your dryer to run longer and hotter. Annual cleaning can save you up to $40 a month.
- **Health Risks** — Blocked vents trap moisture and can prevent proper venting of carbon monoxide from gas dryers — inviting mold.

**Stat callout:** According to the National Fire Protection Association, clothes dryers cause an average of **~16,000 home fires every year**, and **failure to clean** is the leading factor. Don't wait until it's too late.

- **What are the signs of a clogged dryer vent?** — The most common signs are clothes that are still damp after a full cycle, a dryer or laundry room that gets very hot, a burning or musty smell, excess lint around the dryer, or the dryer shutting off mid-cycle.
