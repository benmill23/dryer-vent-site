The Risks of Poor Dryer Vent Maintenance

A clogged dryer vent is more than an inconvenience—it's a serious safety hazard that affects your home, health, and wallet.

Fire Hazard

Lint buildup causes your dryer to overheat, creating a serious fire risk. Dryer fires cause millions in property damage annually.

Reduced Efficiency

About 75% of lint bypasses your lint trap and accumulates in the duct, restricting airflow and increasing drying time.

Higher Energy Bills

A clogged vent forces your dryer to work harder and longer. You can save up to $40/month with annual cleaning.

Health Risks

Blocked vents prevent proper ventilation of carbon monoxide and create conditions for mold growth.

According to the National Fire Protection Association, there are an average of 15,970 home fires each year caused by clothes dryers, with failure to clean being the leading factor.
